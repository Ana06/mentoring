Become an openSUSE 101 Mentor

The world needs more free software hackers/code! We think mentoring is very important for the future of openSUSE. It brings new people to the culture and practices of free software who will enrich our community with their skillsets, ideas and talents.

Being a 101 mentor requires your time, a clear vision of your project and good organization skills. We carefully compiled some tips, tricks and guidelines for mentoring into our mentor HOWTO. Please read this for more information about being a mentor.
